DEVORE INTERCHANGE IMPROVEMENTS

This interchange modernization removed operational bottlenecks at the convergence of I-15 and I-215, a major goods-movement corridor serving Southern California logistics hubs.

Project Details: Owner: Public agency program; Location: SAN BERNARDINO COUNTY, CA; Delivery Method: Project-specific delivery; Status / Completion: Program support.

Our Role: S2 Engineering delivered high-level structural source inspection and Independent Quality Assurance services. A PCI Level III Certified Inspector from S2 oversaw:; Fabrication of prestressed girders; Inspection of complex bent caps; Deck panel production; Pavement slab components Daily coordination occurred with Caltrans Structural Materials Representatives.

Services Provided: PCI Level III Structural Inspection IQA Oversight Fabrication Compliance.

Added Value: S2's certified inspectors ensured high-risk structural components met stringent tolerances and performance requirements, safeguarding long-term durability and load capacity.
