I-80 BRIDGE REPLACEMENTS - PLACER COUNTY, CA (CALTRANS DISTRICT 3)

This SB-1 funded project replaces four aging bridges along Interstate 80 to improve freight mobility, increase clearance for military and commercial vehicles, and enhance public safety.

Project Details: Owner: Public agency program; Location: California; Delivery Method: Project-specific delivery; Status / Completion: Program support.

Our Role: S2 Engineering supports Caltrans Office of Quality Assurance through:; Structural concrete testing and inspection; Pavement materials testing; QA documentation and verification; Specialized materials engineering support; Source inspection coordination for bridge components

Services Provided: Project-specific quality management and inspection services.

Added Value: S2's integrated materials oversight ensured high-risk structural and pavement components met Caltrans performance requirements while keeping critical interstate lanes open during staged construction.
