SBCTA Riverside Avenue Grade Separation Project

This project widened a five-lane bridge to nine lanes and improved Riverside Avenue to meet future I-10 HOV requirements. The project improved traffic flow, accommodated additional lanes, and implemented structural and roadway safety upgrades.

Project Details: Owner: SBCTA / City of Riverside; Location: Riverside, CA; Delivery Method: Design-Bid-Build; Status / Completion: 2022.

Our Role: CM, office engineering, inspection, QA Review and approval of CCOs, RFIs, submittals CPM schedule analysis and monthly reporting Coordination with Caltrans, City, County, and SANBAG

Services Provided: Construction Management & Resident Engineering Materials Testing & QA/QC CPM Scheduling & Reporting Traffic & Stakeholder Coordination Documentation & Claims Support.

Added Value: Minimized delays and construction risks with proactive planning Maintained regulatory compliance for Caltrans and HOV design standards Provided detailed documentation supporting claims resolution
