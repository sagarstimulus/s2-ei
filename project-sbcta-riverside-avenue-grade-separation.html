SBCTA Riverside Avenue Grade Separation Project � Riverside, CA

This project widened a five-lane bridge to nine lanes and improved Riverside Avenue to meet future I-10 HOV requirements. The project improved traffic flow, accommodated additional lanes, and implemented structural and roadway safety upgrades.
