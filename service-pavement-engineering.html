Pavement Engineering

S2 Engineering supports agencies and builders with data-driven pavement engineering that improves performance, ride quality, and service life.

We combine field diagnostics, laboratory interpretation, and engineering judgment to recommend practical pavement solutions. Our team provides support for new construction, rehabilitation, and preservation programs across state and local transportation projects.

Core Services

Evaluation and Diagnostics

  • Inertial profile and smoothness review
  • FWD, coring, and subsurface exploration support
  • DCP and field CBR testing coordination
  • Condition surveys and performance trend analysis

Design and Rehabilitation Support

  • Pavement section recommendations for project needs
  • Constructability and staging impact reviews
  • Preservation strategy planning for lifecycle value
  • Forensic support for distress and premature failures

Where S2 Adds Value

Improved ride quality outcomes
Reduced rework risk during construction
Balanced cost and durability decisions
Clear technical basis for owner approvals
