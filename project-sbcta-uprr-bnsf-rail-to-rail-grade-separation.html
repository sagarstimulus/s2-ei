The San Bernardino County Transportation Authority (SBCTA) initiated the UPRR / BNSF Rail to Rail Grade Separation Project to improve traffic flow, enhance rail safety, and support regional freight and passenger movement. The project involved constructing a new seven-span post-tensioned concrete box girder bridge over an active BNSF railyard and demolishing an existing steel truss bridge. S2 Engineering provided project management and construction management services , including oversight of contractor operations, verification of work quality, and management of project risks.

Project Details

  • Category: Port / Rail / Transit Projects
  • Owner: San Bernardino County Transportation Authority (SBCTA)
  • Location: Barstow, CA
  • Delivery Method: Design-Bid-Build / Alternative Delivery
  • Status / Completion: Ongoing (Construction started 2022, estimated 2024 completion)

Our Role

S2 Engineering provided full project management and construction management , including Oversight and verification of contractor operations and construction quality Review and management of ECRs, RFIs, submittals, CCOs, monthly reports, progress payments, and invoices CPM schedule review including baseline, updates, and 4-week look-ahead Risk management for time, cost, safety, and environmental compliance Coordination of community outreach to minimize public impact Management of construction documentation and record-keeping

Services Performed

  • Project Management and Construction Management Resident and Office Engineering CPM Schedule Review and Progress Monitoring Verification of Contractor Work Product Utility and Stakeholder Coordination Claims Management and Documentation Process Inspection of Bridge Structures, Retaining Walls, and Temporary Traffic Handling

Added Value

Ensured safe construction in a live railyard environment Minimized project delays through proactive schedule and risk management Provided rigorous inspection and quality assurance of complex bridge structures Facilitated coordination between SBCTA, City, and rail operators, ensuring smooth operations and public safety
