City of Barstow - BNSF Yard Grade Separation Project

Constructed a 7-span post-tensioned concrete box girder bridge over BNSF railyard to improve regional connectivity and eliminate at-grade conflicts.

Project Details: Owner: SBCTA / City of Barstow; Location: Barstow, CA; Delivery Method: Design-Bid-Build; Status / Completion: 2024.

Our Role: Program construction management, inspection, QA Risk management, CCO review, CPM schedule monitoring Coordination with rail, utility, and community stakeholders

Services Provided: Construction Management & Resident Engineering Materials Testing & QA/QC CPM Scheduling & Reporting Traffic & Rail Coordination Roadway Construction Projects.

Added Value: Maintained active rail operations safely Delivered high-quality construction with minimal disruption Ensured compliance with Caltrans and Greenbook standards
