Construction Inspection

S2 Engineering delivers inspection services that keep construction quality aligned with design documents, specifications, and owner expectations.

Our inspectors work as an extension of project delivery teams to document compliance, identify issues early, and support timely corrective action. We provide coverage for transportation, bridge, utility, and heavy civil programs in active construction environments.

Core Services

Field Inspection Programs

  • Earthwork, grading, and compaction inspection
  • Paving and structural concrete inspection
  • Bridge and transportation structure oversight
  • Daily inspection reports and issue documentation

Specialized Inspection Support

  • Plant inspection for asphalt, concrete, and aggregates
  • Pavement coring and production verification support
  • Magnetic imaging and alignment verification coordination
  • Portable lab deployment for high-production operations

Where S2 Adds Value

Consistent field coverage and reporting
Early detection of non-compliant work
Reduced claims and rework exposure
Improved coordination between stakeholders
