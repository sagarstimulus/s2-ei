Company Profile

Service Details

S2 Engineering provides specialized services for public infrastructure projects across California. Our two primary service lines are Construction Management and Materials Testing / Source Inspection.

Construction Management

S2 has provided construction management and inspection services for major programs including I-5 North County Enhancement, SR-210 Lane Addition/Baseline Interchange, and Caltrans District 7 On-Call Roadway Construction Inspection support.

Scope Includes

  • Resident Engineering
  • Structure Representative
  • Special Inspection
  • Office Engineering
  • Scheduling
  • Claims Management
  • Project Closeout

Execution Focus

  • Estimate, budget, and work timetable development
  • Contract and technical interpretation for project teams
  • Coordination with architects, engineers, and subcontractors
  • Delay response, issue mitigation, and code compliance

Materials Testing / Source Inspection

S2 has delivered high-quality sampling and testing for Caltrans and public agencies in Districts 4, 6, 7, 8, and 9 for more than two decades, with fixed accredited laboratories serving statewide programs.

Laboratory Locations

  • Laguna Hills, CA
  • Signal Hill, CA
  • Santa Clarita, CA
  • Visalia, CA
  • Fremont, CA

Standards and Capabilities

  • ACI, ASTM, California Tests, AASHTO Test Methods, ASI Standards
  • SIQMP and QAP development
  • IQA / QMA audits and QA/QV support
  • Certified inspectors: PCI, ASNT, AMPP, AWS, ICC
