SBCTA Cherry Avenue Grade Separation Project

The Cherry Avenue project eliminated at-grade rail crossings along a busy corridor, improving traffic flow and public safety. The project involved constructing a new bridge over BNSF tracks and realigning approach roadways while maintaining active rail operations.

Project Details: Owner: SBCTA; Location: San Bernardino, CA; Delivery Method: Design-Bid-Build; Status / Completion: 2022.

Our Role: Comprehensive CM services including inspection, independent QA, resident engineering Office engineering, CCO reviews, monthly reporting, CPM scheduling Coordination with SBCTA, Caltrans, BNSF, and local agencies Claims analysis, documentation, and dispute resolution

Services Provided: Construction Management & Resident Engineering Materials Sampling & Testing QA/QC Documentation & Reporting CPM Scheduling & Look-Ahead Reports Traffic & Public Outreach Coordination.

Added Value: Ensured safety of rail operations while maintaining construction schedule Streamlined QA processes for concrete, HMA, and earthwork Proactive community coordination minimized public impact
