Quality Management & Materials Testing

S2 Engineering has supported Caltrans and local public agencies with materials sampling, testing, and quality management for more than two decades across Districts 4, 6, 7, 8, and 9. Our teams provide dependable test data and compliance reporting that supports acceptance decisions and long-term pavement performance.

We operate Caltrans and AASHTO-accredited lab programs and coordinated field teams to deliver end-to-end quality support including sampling, batch plant inspection, QC/QA verification testing, and mix design confirmation.

Quality and Testing Scope

  • Field and laboratory materials testing for soil, aggregate, asphalt, and concrete
  • Quality assurance and quality control (QA/QC) oversight across all project phases
  • Sampling, inspection, and verification to ensure compliance with project specifications
  • Monitoring of material performance and identification of non-conformances
  • Coordination with contractors and agencies for quality alignment
  • Accurate documentation and timely reporting to support project decisions and approvals
