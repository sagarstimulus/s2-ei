Testing Services

S2 Engineering provides laboratory and field testing services that deliver accurate data, dependable turnaround, and confidence for critical infrastructure decisions.

Our testing teams support transportation and public works programs across California with accredited methods, experienced personnel, and responsive deployment. We deliver both scheduled and rapid-response testing support for active construction operations.

Core Services

Laboratory Testing

  • Soils and aggregate property testing
  • Concrete, asphalt, and masonry laboratory testing
  • Asphalt binder and mix performance support
  • Pavement preservation material test programs

Field Testing and Verification

  • In-place density, moisture, and compaction testing
  • Concrete and asphalt sampling for acceptance
  • Independent verification and quality monitoring support
  • Mobile laboratory deployment for remote or high-volume sites

Where S2 Adds Value

Accredited methods and consistent quality
Fast turnaround for construction decisions
Reliable support for agency acceptance
Statewide coverage through fixed and mobile labs
