I-5 LA North County Enhancement Project

This large-scale Design-Build project by LA Metro and Caltrans aimed to expand lanes and dedicated truck traffic lanes along I-5 through Valencia and Santa Clarita. Improvements included bridge widening, soundwall construction, and extensive roadway rehabilitation.

Project Details: Owner: LA Metro / Caltrans District 7; Location: Santa Clarita, CA; Delivery Method: Design-Build; Status / Completion: Ongoing.

Our Role: Provided quality management, material testing, and plant inspection services Supervised field testers, plant inspectors, and lab testing for concrete, aggregates, and asphalt Task order management to review and submit deliverables from material testers Daily reporting and verification of QA compliance

Services Provided: Material Testing & QA Inspection Batch Plant Inspection Concrete, Aggregate & Asphalt Testing QA Reporting & Documentation.

Added Value: Real-time reporting allowed immediate detection of fabrication or construction issues Maintained consistent quality and construction progress across multiple work fronts Reduced rework and improved schedule adherence
