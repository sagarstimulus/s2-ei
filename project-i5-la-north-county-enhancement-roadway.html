I-5 LA North County Enhancement Project � Santa Clarita, CA

This large-scale Design-Build project by LA Metro and Caltrans aimed to expand lanes and dedicated truck traffic lanes along I-5 through Valencia and Santa Clarita. Improvements included bridge widening, soundwall construction, and extensive roadway rehabilitation.

Project Details

  • Category: Consultant Construction Management & Inspection Projects
  • Owner: LA Metro / Caltrans District 7
  • Location: Santa Clarita, CA
  • Delivery Method: Design-Build
  • Status / Completion: Ongoing
