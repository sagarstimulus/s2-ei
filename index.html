WHO WE ARE

Established in 2001, S2 Engineering is a certified DBE firm specializing in construction management, inspection services, and materials testing for public works projects across California. Our reputation is built on delivering high-quality, professional services with precision, reliability, and a deep understanding of project dynamics.

At the core of our business is a commitment to excellence. We pride ourselves on our ability to anticipate project needs and proactively address challenges, ensuring each project progresses smoothly and successfully. Our experienced team works closely with stakeholders to fully understand their objectives, enabling us to provide tailored solutions that meet or exceed expectations.

What We Do

S2 Engineering is dedicated to partnering with owners, contractors, design-builders, and project delivery teams to effectively provide Quality Management, Pavement Engineering, Materials Design, Construction Inspection, and Testing Services.

Construction Management

Full-service construction management from preconstruction through project closeout for transportation and public works.

  • Resident engineering and office engineering support
  • Scheduling, claims mitigation, and issue resolution
  • Agency and stakeholder coordination
View Service Details

Quality Management & Materials Testing

Integrated QA/QC programs with accredited field and laboratory testing for concrete, asphalt, aggregates, and soils.

  • Lab testing and field density/compaction programs
  • Concrete, asphalt, and aggregate verification
  • Independent testing documentation and reporting
View Service Details

Source Inspection Services

METS-based source inspection services for fabrication plants, structural materials, and project-specific quality plans.

  • SIQMP, QAP, IQA/QMA audit, and QA/QV support
  • Structural materials representative services
  • Multi-discipline certified inspection staff
View Service Details

Additional Service Areas

Expanded technical support tailored to agency and project delivery needs beyond core service lines.

  • Pavement engineering and materials design
  • QA/QC oversight and quality validation roles
  • Public agency support and compliance coordination
