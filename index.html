Services

Our service lines are built around Construction Management and Materials Testing / Source Inspection.

Construction Management

S2 Engineering provides construction management and construction inspection services on public works projects throughout California, including I-5 North County Enhancement, SR-210 Lane Addition/Baseline Interchange, and District 7 On-Call Roadway Construction Inspection services.

  • Resident Engineering
  • Structure Representative
  • Special Inspection
  • Office Engineering
  • Scheduling and Claims Management
  • Project Closeout

Materials Testing / Source Inspection

S2 has provided materials sampling and testing for Caltrans and other agencies across Districts 4, 6, 7, 8, and 9 for more than two decades, supported by Caltrans and AASHTO-accredited laboratories.

  • Testing support: ACI, ASTM, California Tests, AASHTO Test Methods, ASI Standards
  • Source Inspection Quality Management Plan (SIQMP) development
  • Structural Materials Representative and QAP development
  • IQA / QMA audits and QA/QV support
  • Certifications including PCI, ASNT, AMPP, AWS, ICC
