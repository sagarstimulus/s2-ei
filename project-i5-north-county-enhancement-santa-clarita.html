I-5 NORTH COUNTY ENHANCEMENT PROJECT (LA METRO / CALTRANS)

This flagship freeway expansion is Southern California's largest Design-Bid-Build highway construction project, delivering new general-purpose lanes, dedicated truck lanes, bridge replacements, retaining walls, and extensive pavement systems along the I-5 corridor.

Project Details: Owner: Public agency program; Location: SANTA CLARITA, CA; Delivery Method: Project-specific delivery; Status / Completion: Program support.

Our Role: S2 Engineering serves as Prime Consultant for Quality Management and Materials Testing providing:; Project Quality Manager and Quality Engineers; Field testers, laboratory technicians, and plant inspectors; Continuous materials testing for concrete, asphalt, aggregates, and earthwork; Mix design review and compliance verification; QA documentation and deliverable management; Round-the-clock quality coverage across all work fronts

Services Provided: Project-specific quality management and inspection services.

Added Value: S2's integrated quality program maintains consistent material performance across massive production volumes while supporting accelerated construction schedules. Early detection of mix variability and placement issues has prevented rework and protected long-term pavement durability.
