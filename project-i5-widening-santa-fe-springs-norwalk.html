I-5 Widening Project

The I-5 Widening Project improved corridor capacity, safety, and operational efficiency through densely urbanized areas of Los Angeles County. The project involved replacing an existing two-lane steel bridge with a 10-lane concrete structure and extensive utility relocation.

Project Details: Owner: Caltrans District 7; Location: Santa Fe Springs / Norwalk, CA; Delivery Method: Design-Bid-Build; Status / Completion: 2017.

Our Role: Provided CM and QA inspection services CPM schedule review and claims analysis Oversight of construction, material testing, and office engineering Coordination with Union Pacific Railroad for right-of-way work

Services Provided: Construction Management & QA Inspection Materials Testing & Field Inspection CPM Scheduling & Claims Support Utility & Railroad Coordination.

Added Value: Delivered project in compliance with Caltrans standards Minimized delays due to utility and railroad coordination Ensured high-quality construction with rigorous QA processes
