Construction Management

S2 Engineering provides construction management and construction inspection services for public works and transportation projects across California. We partner with owners, contractors, and delivery teams to maintain schedule control, quality compliance, and cost accountability from preconstruction through final acceptance.

Our team has delivered major corridor and interchange projects including LA Metro I-5 North County Enhancement, SBCTA SR-210 Lane Addition and Baseline Interchange, and Caltrans District 7 on-call roadway construction inspection assignments. We apply proven project controls and practical field coordination to keep work moving and avoid downstream disputes.

Detailed Duties

Planning and Cost Control

  • Prepare cost estimates, budgets, and work timetables
  • Develop baseline schedules and recovery plans
  • Coordinate contractor resources and subcontractor sequencing
  • Track earned progress against project milestones

Contract Administration

  • Interpret and explain contracts and technical requirements
  • Manage RFIs, submittals, and change documentation
  • Report progress and budget status to clients and agencies
  • Support claims analysis and entitlement records

Technical Coordination

  • Collaborate with architects, engineers, and specialists
  • Coordinate utility, traffic stage, and stakeholder interfaces
  • Respond to work delays, field conflicts, and emergencies
  • Maintain project momentum through issue-resolution meetings

Compliance and Closeout

  • Enforce legal requirements and safety/code compliance
  • Coordinate punch list completion and final acceptance
  • Confirm as-builts, quantity reconciliation, and turnover records
  • Deliver final closeout documentation for owner use
