Construction Management

S2 Engineering provides construction management and construction inspection services for public works and transportation projects across California. We partner with owners, contractors, and delivery teams to maintain schedule control, quality compliance, cost accountability, schedule control, Safety, and environmental compliance from preconstruction through final acceptance.

We provide end-to-end project support including planning and cost control, scheduling, contract administration, technical coordination, and project closeout. Our team ensures effective communication, regulatory compliance, and timely delivery while proactively resolving issues to keep projects on track from start to finish.
