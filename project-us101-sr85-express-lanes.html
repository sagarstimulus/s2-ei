US-101 / SR-85 EXPRESS LANES PROJECT

This major congestion-relief project converted existing HOV lanes into express lanes across two of Silicon Valley's busiest freeways. The project introduced new overhead sign structures, foundation systems, traffic management infrastructure, and roadway improvements to support real-time toll operations.

Project Details: Owner: Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority with Caltrans Oversight; Location: Santa Clara County, CA; Delivery Method: Project-specific delivery; Status / Completion: 2016.

Our Role: S2 Engineering provided quality assurance oversight for materials testing and structural source inspection. Key responsibilities included:; Development and approval of the project Source Inspection Quality Plan; Inspection of fabricated steel sign structures; Verification of welding operations and bolt assemblies; Oversight of concrete testing and foundation materials; Periodic QA audits of contractor testing activities

Services Provided: Structural Source Inspection QA Oversight Materials Testing Verification.

Added Value: S2 ensured full Caltrans procedural compliance on a locally administered project, bridging the gap between agency standards and contractor production efficiency.
