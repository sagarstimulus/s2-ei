About Us

S2 Engineering is a California-based infrastructure partner delivering construction management, inspection, and materials engineering services for complex public works programs.

Established in 2001, S2 Engineering is a certified DBE firm built around technical quality, dependable field delivery, and responsive client support. We work across transportation corridors, bridges, rail, transit, and public agency capital programs.

Our teams integrate with owners, contractors, and design-build partners to keep projects aligned on scope, quality requirements, and schedule performance. From preconstruction planning through closeout, we focus on clear communication and practical solutions that keep work moving.

Every assignment is supported by disciplined reporting, experienced staff, and a commitment to protecting public investment through accurate documentation and proven engineering judgment.
