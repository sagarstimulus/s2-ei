About Us

Established in 2001, S2 Engineering is a certified Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) firm committed to technical quality, dependable field delivery, and responsive client support. We work across transportation corridors, bridges, rail, transit, and public agency capital programs.

Our team seamlessly integrates with owners, contractors, and design-build partners to keep projects aligned with scope, quality standards, and schedule milestones. From preconstruction planning through project closeout, we emphasize clear communication and practical solutions to keep work moving forward.

Every task is supported by disciplined reporting, experienced staff, and a steadfast commitment to protecting public investments through sound engineering judgment and accurate documentation.

Our Mission

S2 Engineering is dedicated to serving the needs of public and private sector clients by providing professional, timely, cost-effective and high-quality tailored construction management, materials testing, quality management and civil engineering services, while prioritizing safety and environmental commitments. S2 Engineering accomplishes this by creating a team of people based upon experience, commitment, service, responsiveness, leadership, and innovation.
