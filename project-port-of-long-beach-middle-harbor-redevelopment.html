Port of Long Beach � Middle Harbor Redevelopment Phases 2 & 3

The Middle Harbor Redevelopment Project at the Port of Long Beach represents a transformative upgrade to one of the world�s busiest container ports. Phases 2 and 3 included the construction of state-of-the-art Pier E Terminal Container Yard/Intermodal Railyard and Berth E22 Wharf and Backlands , enhancing cargo handling capacity, operational efficiency, and automation. The redevelopment supports modern shipping operations with advanced cranes, automated guided vehicles, and on-dock rail capabilities, aligning with global logistics demands. S2 Engineering provided office engineering, document control, and support for construction management on this multi-billion-dollar project.

  • Owner: Port of Long Beach
  • Location: Long Beach, CA
  • Delivery Method: Design-Build
