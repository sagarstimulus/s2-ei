I-10 Live Oak Canyon Interchange Project

The Live Oak Canyon Interchange Project widened I-10 and upgraded a key interchange to improve regional traffic flow and accommodate heavy truck volumes. The project involved complex ramp modifications and extensive retaining wall and drainage improvements.

Project Details: Owner: SANBAG / Caltrans District 8; Location: Redlands, CA; Delivery Method: Design-Bid-Build; Status / Completion: 2015.

Our Role: CM, QA inspection, and resident engineering Materials testing for concrete, asphalt, and soils CPM schedule review and reporting Traffic and public coordination

Services Provided: Construction Management & Resident Engineering Materials Testing & QA CPM Scheduling & Reporting Traffic Coordination.

Added Value: Minimized construction disruptions to commuters Ensured quality compliance and safety Proactively managed construction schedule and contractor performance
