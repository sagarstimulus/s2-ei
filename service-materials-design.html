Materials Design

S2 Engineering develops project-specific material designs that support durability, constructability, and long-term infrastructure performance.

Our materials specialists support owners and contractors with mix development, laboratory validation, and production readiness reviews. We tailor designs to project requirements, agency criteria, and local material availability to maintain quality through construction.

Core Services

Mix and Material Development

  • Hot mix asphalt and pavement preservation mix design support
  • Concrete trial batching and proportion optimization
  • Aggregate source qualification and gradation balance
  • Soil-cement, CTB, and full-depth reclamation recommendations

Validation and Delivery Readiness

  • Performance validation against project specifications
  • Production startup monitoring and first-run verification
  • Documentation packages for owner and agency review
  • Ongoing technical adjustments for changing field conditions
