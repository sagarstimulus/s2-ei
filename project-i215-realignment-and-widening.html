I-215 Realignment and Widening Project � San Bernardino, CA

The I-215 Realignment Project addressed congestion in San Bernardino by improving capacity, safety, and operations along a key regional corridor. The project included realigning lanes, reconstructing interchanges, and widening segments to accommodate future traffic demands.

Project Details

  • Category: Consultant Construction Management & Inspection Projects
  • Owner: SANBAG / Caltrans District 8
  • Location: San Bernardino, CA
  • Delivery Method: Design-Bid-Build
  • Status / Completion: 2010
