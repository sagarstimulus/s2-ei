I-215 Realignment and Widening Project

The I-215 Realignment Project addressed congestion in San Bernardino by improving capacity, safety, and operations along a key regional corridor. The project included realigning lanes, reconstructing interchanges, and widening segments to accommodate future traffic demands.

Project Details: Owner: SANBAG / Caltrans District 8; Location: San Bernardino, CA; Delivery Method: Design-Bid-Build; Status / Completion: 2010.

Our Role: CM, QA inspection, and resident engineering Materials testing of concrete, HMA, and soils CPM schedule review and monthly reporting Coordination with Caltrans, city, and utility stakeholders

Services Provided: Construction Management & Resident Engineering Materials Sampling & Testing CPM Scheduling & Reporting Traffic & Utility Coordination QA/QC Documentation.

Added Value: Enabled safe traffic flow during construction Proactively managed schedule and material quality Provided detailed documentation for claims and compliance
